About Us

A football team is a group of players selected to play together in the various team sports known as football.

Welcome to Club

Started in 2008 with the aim of developing and changing the grassroots landscape in Navi Mumbai .SPFA has come a long way . SPFA focuses on the all round development of the child, nourishes and nurtures them to become the next stalwarts of the beautiful game. We have honed some of the finest current talents of football (Rahul Bheke player (Indian National team & Bengaluru FC),Mr. Nikhil Poojari player (Indian National team & Pune FC).Our coaches are veteran players with deep comprehension about the game coupled with modern coaching techniques and infrastructure . We firmly stand by our motto" shaping future ......
