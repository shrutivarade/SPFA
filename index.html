GOA Exposure Tour

(Since 2008)

The tournament was started back in 2008 with a view to give kids the right exposure.The ground zero of the tournament is to get the young footballer acquainted with proffesional football setup. More than 30 teams across India participate every year in this tournament. Goa's affinity and rich history of the beautiful game makes it a perfect host for the tournament.

SPFA Grassroot development League

(Since 2013)

The academy believes in the phrase "catch them young" and hence hosts a baby leaugue for the youngest footballers of the pack with collaboration of western india football association. The academy sees to it that sportsmanship and fairplay is instilled in the young impressionable minds.
